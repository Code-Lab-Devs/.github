# Code Lab - Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge
Welcome to **Code Lab** at the University of Tripoli! We are a student-led community dedicated to learning, sharing, and growing as IT professionals. 

We pledge to make participation in our club and our GitHub organization a harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of skill level, major, gender, or background. Whether you are writing your first line of code or deploying complex apps, you belong here.

## Our Standards
Examples of behavior that contributes to a positive environment include:
*   Using welcoming and inclusive language.
*   Being respectful of differing viewpoints and experiences (especially during Code Reviews).
*   Gracefully accepting constructive criticism.
*   Focusing on what is best for the community and the learning process.
*   Showing empathy towards other students and beginners.

Examples of unacceptable behavior include:
*   Trolling, insulting/derogatory comments, and personal or political attacks.
*   Public or private harassment.
*   Publishing others' private information without explicit permission.
*   Mocking others for "bad code" or lack of knowledge. We are here to learn!

## Enforcement & Reporting
The **Code Lab Admin Team** is responsible for clarifying the standards of acceptable behavior and will take appropriate and fair corrective action in response to any instances of unacceptable behavior.

If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior, please report it by contacting the organization Admins directly via Telegram. All complaints will be reviewed and investigated promptly and fairly.
